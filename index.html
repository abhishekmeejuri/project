CLINICAL SPECIALITY

MULTI ORGAN TRANSPLANTATION

Organ transplantation is a medical procedure in which an organ is removed from one body and placed in the body of a recipient, to replace a damaged or missing organ. The donor and recipient may be at the same location, or organs may be transported from a donor site to another location. Organ donors may be living, brain-dead, or dead via circulatory death.
